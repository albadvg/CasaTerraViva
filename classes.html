This class, 1   1/2 hours long, seeks to balance the educational and the fun sides of tasting.
It has 3 objectives:

  • teach how to describe a wine, that is, verbalize our sensations
  • have fun through various tasting games and exercises
  • familiarize ourselves with different wines so we can decide which we like best.

Classes are open to everyone, regardless of whether they are staying at the house. Classes take place in the house next to the house for rent. Occupancy will be limited to 5 people and a safe distance between attendants will be enforced.
The contents of the class will be changing throughout the year. Here we present the contents and dates of the class being currently offered.

Galician White Wines
CONTENTS
  1. How to describe wine: The aroma wheel
  2. Tasting games: "Inverse tasting"
  3. Knowing the facts to enhance our enjoyment: The Galician D.O.s
  4. Hedonic and descriptive tastings of three representative Galician wines
