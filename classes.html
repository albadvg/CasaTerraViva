This class, 1   1/2 hours long, seeks to balance the educational and the fun sides of tasting.
It has 3 objectives:

  • teach how to describe a wine, that is, verbalize our sensations
  • have fun through various tasting games and exercises
  • familiarize ourselves with different wines so we can decide which we like best.

Wine tasting classes are open to everyone, regardless of whether they are staying at the house. Classes take place in the house next to the house for rent. Occupancy is limited to 8 people. The contents of the class will be changing throughout the year. Here we present the contents and dates of the class being currently offered.
Classes could be arranged on additional dates based on host availability. Classes can be offered in English or Spanish.
To sign up for a scheduled class or to request a class on alternative dates contact the host at:
