The house today

The rental unit, 81 m2 (872 ft2) is a complete dwelling on the East side of an old farmhouse, with private entrance both to the house and to the yard. The presence in the house of iron plows and even wooden ones, as well as the instruments to weave the flax, are a testimony that the house is at least 180 years old. The house has two sleeping quarters - one with a double bed and a bathroom, the other with a sofa-bed and no bath -, a fully-equipped kitchen/dining room, a living room with TV and a wood-burning stove, a laundry room with a washing machine and a washing tub, a patio with a gas barbeque for private use, an horreo (granary) for the kids to play, and a yard of approximately 400 m2 (4300 ft2) also for private use.
